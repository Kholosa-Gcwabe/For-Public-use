About FPU

Started with zero capital. Built on pre-orders. Designed for public use.

FPU is a creative studio that happens to make clothes. Or maybe it's a clothing label that happens to build websites. Either way, we believe good design should be accessible—to wear, to use, and to hire.

We launched Drop 001 to prove we could fund our own production through community support. Every pre-order goes straight into manufacturing. No waste. No excess inventory.

That same community-first approach drives our client work. We build brands and digital experiences for people who need them—startups, small businesses, and creators who can't afford agency prices but deserve agency quality.
